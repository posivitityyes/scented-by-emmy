Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to already own a lot of perfumes?

Not at all. I work with beginners and collectors. We’ll build from wherever you are — even if you only own one bottle right now.

Will you only recommend super expensive fragrances?

No. I work within your budget. That includes affordable fragrances, perfume oils, and sometimes dupes — the focus is on what suits you, not the price tag.

How do we meet for the session?

Sessions are done over phone or video call. After you book, you’ll receive a confirmation email with the link and a short intake form.

Will you send me actual samples?

I don’t ship samples at this time, but I’ll recommend where to buy decants, discovery sets, or sample packs so you can test before committing to full bottles.

Do you work with brides or special events?

Yes. I offer custom bridal and special event scent styling. Reach out via the form below and mention your date and vision.
