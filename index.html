FAQ

Do I need a big perfume collection?

No. We can start with one bottle or a whole shelf. I meet you where you are.

Will you only suggest expensive scents?

I work within your budget. That can include affordable scents, oils, and dupes.

How do we meet?

By phone or video. After you book, I send your call details and a short form.

Do you send samples?

I don’t ship samples, but I’ll point you to decants and discovery sets to try first.

Do you work with brides?

Yes. Bridal and special event scent styling is available — just mention it in your message.
