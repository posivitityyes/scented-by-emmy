Meet Emmy

Emmy is the friend everyone texts with, “What should I wear tonight?” After years of studying perfumes and oils, testing combinations on herself and her friends, and diving deep into fragrance education, she turned her obsession into a service.

Her goal is simple: help you create a scent identity that feels like you — soft, bold, romantic, playful, or all of the above.
